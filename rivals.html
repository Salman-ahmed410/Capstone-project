Rivals

Rivals is currently one of the best shooter game in roblox,many players
are bad at game and want to improve ,this guide will show you how to be
good at Rivals.

Bad movement is what makes many players bad at Rivals, to fix this you need to slide jump
its when u slide with the button(or you can find the controls in the settings)and then jump
this makes it hard for enemies to aim at you.

Bad aim is another factor to fix this,you need to use a aim trainer like aimlabs which you
get from steam and is very good.Also during matches stay calm as being tensed will ruin your
aim.

Bad choice of weapons is also a factor,for long range maps like docks and crossroads use a sniper
and uzi,for small maps use shotgun or rpg but an assault rifle is better as nothing can go wrong with
also for assault rifle player a high sensitivity is recommended while a revolver user should use low
sensitivity for good aim.
